INTER SCHOOL COMPETITIONS
ROTARY CLUB - SPIRITS

Rotary Club, Navi Mumbai Seaside organises Inter School Youth Festival – ‘SPIRITS’ every year which witnesses participation from several schools of Navi Mumbai in variety of events viz. music, dance, skit, drawing, art and craft, speech, writing skills. SBOA Public School has been its regular participant and the proud winner of its Championship Trophy for three consecutive years.
