SBOA Public School (Nerul) is a successful and well established school with an excellent reputation in the field of education. High academic standards, wonderful sporting performances and outstanding achievements in different arenas have made its name become synonymous with excellence in education.

The school was established in the year 2003 by the SBIOA Education Society, a non-profit making organization with the goal to achieve high quality in schooling young minds. Founded in 1965, the society which started with its first school at Annanagar, Chennai has now grown into a family of 16 schools

SBOA Public School, Nerul, a branch to the family tree of the SBIOA Society is situated in the heart of Navi Mumbai with the sprawling premises of 3500sq mtr. The school is affiliated to the State Board of Maharashtra and the medium of instruction here is English. The school is segmented into three categories –

  • Pre Primary: Nursery to Senior KG

  • Primary: I to VII

  • Secondary: VIII to X
