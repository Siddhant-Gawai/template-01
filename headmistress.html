  • From the Headmistress
“Learning gives creativity
Creativity leads to thinking
Thinking provides knowledge
Knowledge makes you great”
Dr. A P J Abdul Kalam

Welcome to SBOA Public School, Nerul where learning kindles the creativity of students and gives them the freedom and the opportunities to deepen their thinking and widen their knowledge.
Children are like buds in the garden and should be carefully and lovingly nurtured as they are the future of the nation. Here at SBOA we continuously work in this direction by providing value based education laying special emphasis on character building and upholding high moral values.
We promote these core values of the school among our students by driving them forward into new initiatives both academic and co-curricular.
We take pride in our outstanding academic results and exemplary child care. Everything we do comes from the heart. Our unique achievement lies in creating an institution which is like a home away from home for our students. Our dedicated teachers consider this institution as their extended family. Thus there is empathy and sensitivity in every action they do thereby creating a healthy and conducive learning environment.
Please do visit our school to know more about us as to how we transform young boys and girls into responsible citizens of tomorrow with love and care.

- Mrs. Kalpana Rishi
